Key Features
Here's a comprehensive breakdown of its main capabilities:

Song Import & Management:
Import from ChordPro, OnSong, PDF (including Word documents in some mentions), or directly search and pull songs from supported sites like UltimateGuitar.com, WorshipTogether.com, and e-chords.com inside the app.
Includes a free SongbookPro Manager desktop tool for bulk importing, live editing with preview pane, organizing, and managing the library without constantly transferring files.
Organize songs into sets (setlists), folders, or custom groupings; sort in multiple ways; searchable library.

Display & Performance Tools:
Clean, concise display of chords above lyrics (or full sheet music via PDF).
Automatic scrolling or page turning (adjustable by tempo/BPM).
Quick transposition to any key and easy capo indicators.
Integrated metronome with tap-to-set tempo; it remembers tempos between songs.
Some mentions of chord diagrams (e.g., for guitar/ukulele) and optional backing track playback from device library or Spotify.

Customization:
Fully customizable interface: dark theme, color-code sections (e.g., make all choruses purple), highlight chords (e.g., yellow), etc.
Adjustable layouts, zoom, and fonts for optimal reading on tablets/laptops.

Annotations & Notes:
Freehand drawing directly on the music using stylus, Apple Pencil, or finger (20 colors, highlighter mode, undo support).
Annotations intelligently stay linked to the correct part of the song even if layout, zoom, or transposition changes.
Typed text notes that can appear as overlays or at the start of a song.

Sharing & Sync:
Sync personal song libraries across all your devices (including desktop via Manager).
SongbookPro Groups for sharing and maintaining a common library with band members or groups.
Simple sharing of individual songs or entire sets with other Songbook Pro users.
Bluetooth pedal support for hands-free page turning.

Other Practical Tools:
Setlist creation for live gigs with easy song switching.
Tempo/duration settings to sync scrolling with playback.
Optional features like a drone note for the song's key.


Target Users & Benefits
It's especially popular with solo musicians, bands, pianists, guitarists, and church/worship teams who need quick access to charts during rehearsals or performances. Users highlight how it organizes messy libraries, improves practice, eliminates hauling heavy binders, and supports both chord charts and traditional sheet music.
The app is free to try (limited to 12 songs and no sync) with a one-time in-app purchase to unlock the full version.
Relevance for Another Web App
If you're building a web-based alternative (e.g., a browser-first songbook or music collaboration tool), Songbook Pro demonstrates strong demand for:

Seamless multi-device sync and group sharing.
Rich import options from popular formats and online sources.
Live-performance optimizations (scrolling, pedals, metronome, quick transpose).
Deep customization and annotation layers that adapt intelligently.
A companion desktop manager for power users.
